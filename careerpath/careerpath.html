Science Stream

For students interested in technology, medicine, research, and innovation.

Engineering (B.Tech/B.E)

Engineers design, build, and maintain engines, machines, and structures. Specializations include Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, and Electrical engineering.

Medicine (MBBS/BDS)

Doctors diagnose and treat illnesses. It requires dedication and long study hours but offers a rewarding career in saving lives.

Pure Sciences & Research

Scientists conduct experiments to understand the natural world. This path is ideal for those with a curious mind and a passion for discovery.

Data Science & AI

Data scientists analyze complex data to help make decisions. AI specialists build intelligent systems. This is a rapidly growing field.

Commerce Stream

For students interested in business, finance, economics, and management.

Chartered Accountancy (CA)

CAs manage financial accounts, auditing, and taxation. It is a prestigious qualification for finance professionals.

Business Management (BBA/MBA)

Managers oversee operations and lead teams. An MBA opens doors to leadership roles in various industries.

Investment Banking & Finance

Investment bankers help companies raise capital and make financial decisions. It is a high-pressure, high-reward career.

Economics

Economists study the production and distribution of resources. They work in government, banking, and academia.

Arts & Humanities Stream

For students interested in creativity, social sciences, law, and media.

Law (LLB)

Lawyers advise and represent clients in legal matters. It requires strong analytical and communication skills.

Journalism & Mass Communication

Journalists report news and stories. They work in print, broadcast, and digital media.

Graphic & Fashion Design

Designers create visual concepts. Fields include graphic design, fashion design, and interior design.

Psychology

Psychologists study human behavior and mental processes. They work in clinical settings, schools, and organizations.
